Range of Dental Services and Treatments in Kenmore

Kenmore Dentists has been providing trusted dental services to customers for over ten years. Under the direction of Dr Simon Akhtar, our team are trained in all areas of dental health and hygiene, with a strong focus on customer service.

Flexible Appointments

At Kenmore Dentists, we know that life can get busy and hectic. That’s why our appointments are very accommodating and can be organised to fit in with your schedule. We offer appointments during the day as well as late nights and on Saturday.

Caring and Supportive Staff
